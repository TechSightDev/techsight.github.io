A structured approach that preserves velocity while reducing complexity.

The Challenge

Why Technical Integration Fails

Most acquisitions destroy value in the integration phase — not because the technology was bad, but because the integration was poorly planned. Rushed timelines, conflicting architectures, and culture clashes turn a strategic acquisition into an operational burden.

Velocity Collapse

When integration disrupts both teams' ability to ship, feature development stalls. Customers see delayed roadmaps, and the combined company loses the momentum that justified the deal.

Talent Flight

Engineering talent leaves when integration feels chaotic, decisions feel arbitrary, or their domain expertise is undervalued. Losing key engineers often means losing critical institutional knowledge.

Integration Debt

Quick-fix integrations that "just make it work" create long-lived technical debt — duplicate systems, fragile bridges, and operational overhead that compound for years.

What We Deliver

A Structured Path to Unified Operations

Integration Architecture Plan

A detailed plan for converging two technology stacks — which systems to merge, which to sunset, which to run in parallel, and in what sequence. Every decision is informed by business impact and engineering effort.

Infrastructure Consolidation

Strategy and execution plan for consolidating cloud accounts, CI/CD pipelines, monitoring, and operational tooling. We prioritize quick wins that reduce costs and complexity while maintaining stability.

Data & API Integration

Plan for unifying data stores, APIs, and shared services. We design clean integration boundaries that allow incremental migration without big-bang cutovers or extended freeze periods.

Development Workflow Unification

Alignment of development processes — source control, code review, testing, deployment, and on-call practices. We find common ground that respects both teams' strengths rather than imposing a one-size-fits-all approach.

Team Structure & Retention Plan

Recommendations for organizational structure, role alignment, knowledge transfer, and retention strategies for key engineers. We focus on preserving the capabilities you acquired.

Phased Integration Roadmap

A sequenced plan with milestones, dependencies, and decision gates. Designed to deliver integration value incrementally while keeping both products stable and both teams shipping.
