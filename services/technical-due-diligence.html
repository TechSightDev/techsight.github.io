Technical Ground Truth for Acquisition Decisions

Acquisitions live or die on what's under the hood. You get the ground truth you need to negotiate with confidence and plan integration accurately.

The Problem

Why Technical Diligence Gets Shortchanged

Financial and legal due diligence are standard. But technical diligence is often cursory — a surface-level architecture review that misses the real risks buried in the code, infrastructure, and team dynamics. Most advisors interview the engineering team and call it done. We read the code.

Hidden Technical Debt

A product can look polished on the surface while carrying years of accumulated shortcuts, deprecated dependencies, and fragile architecture. This debt becomes your problem post-acquisition.

Inflated Capabilities

Sales decks describe what the product does. Due diligence reveals how it does it — and whether the architecture can actually deliver on the roadmap you're acquiring.

Integration Surprises

Without a deep technical assessment, integration costs are guesswork. Incompatible tech stacks, undocumented dependencies, and bus-factor risks can turn a good deal into a costly one.

What We Deliver

A Comprehensive Technical Assessment

Codebase & Architecture Review

Deep analysis of code quality, architectural patterns, test coverage, dependency health, and technical debt. We assess whether the codebase is a foundation to build on or a liability to manage.

Infrastructure & DevOps Assessment

Evaluation of cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, monitoring, disaster recovery, and operational maturity. We identify fragilities, single points of failure, and scalability constraints.

Security & Compliance Posture

Assessment of security practices, access controls, data handling, encryption, vulnerability management, and compliance status. Critical for understanding regulatory and reputational risk.

Team & Knowledge Assessment

Evaluation of team structure, key-person dependencies, documentation quality, and knowledge distribution. We identify retention risks and capability gaps that affect post-acquisition execution.

Scalability & Performance Analysis

Assessment of current load handling, performance bottlenecks, and architectural limits. We model whether the system can grow with your plans or will require significant re-engineering.

Risk Register & Remediation Costs

A prioritized register of technical risks with severity, likelihood, and estimated remediation costs. Designed to inform your negotiation and integration planning directly.
