Available on a retainer that scales with your stage and needs.

The Problem

The Leadership Gap

Growing B2B SaaS companies hit a point where technical decisions have strategic consequences — which database to bet on, when to refactor the monolith, whether to build or buy AI capabilities — but the team lacks a senior technical leader to navigate them. The result is drift: incremental decisions that individually seem fine but collectively lead the technology in the wrong direction. And hiring a full-time CTO is expensive and hard to justify before Series B.

Expensive Missteps

Without experienced technical leadership, teams make architecture decisions that seem reasonable today but create expensive problems at scale — the wrong database, a monolith that should be services, or vice versa.

Strategy Disconnect

When technical decisions happen in isolation from business strategy, you end up building the wrong things well. A CTO bridges the gap between what the business needs and what the engineering team builds.

Full-Time Cost Barrier

A seasoned CTO commands significant compensation. For many companies — especially pre-Series B — the full-time cost isn't justified, but the need for the expertise is real and growing.
