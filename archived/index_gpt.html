Our Services

At TechSight, we specialize in providing expert guidance and solutions that help you make informed decisions during critical business transitions. Our range of services is designed to deliver actionable insights and optimize your technology investments.

M&A Technical Due Diligence

We conduct comprehensive technical due diligence for mergers and acquisitions, ensuring that you understand the true value and risks of technology assets. Our in-depth assessments cover code quality, architecture, scalability, and more, helping you make informed investment decisions.

Software Consulting

Our consulting services focus on optimizing your software development processes and architecture. Whether you’re building a new product or enhancing an existing one, we provide the expertise needed to streamline operations, reduce costs, and accelerate time to market.

Post-Acquisition Optimization

After an acquisition, we help you integrate and optimize the acquired technology assets. Our services include system integration, cost optimization, and operational efficiency improvements, ensuring a smooth transition and maximizing your return on investment.
